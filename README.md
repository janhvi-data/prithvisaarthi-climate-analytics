## Overview

PrithviSaarthi is a climate data analytics platform developed to analyze global environmental trends using interactive Power BI dashboards and data visualization techniques. The project focuses on climate awareness through analytics-driven storytelling by transforming complex environmental datasets into meaningful insights.

The dashboard provides analysis on greenhouse gas emissions, carbon dioxide concentration, global temperature rise, renewable energy adoption, and sea level changes to understand long-term climate patterns and sustainability trends.

---

## Business Problem

Climate datasets are large, complex, and difficult to interpret without proper visualization. Organizations and researchers require interactive analytical platforms to monitor environmental changes, understand sustainability trends, and identify key climate indicators affecting global ecosystems.

PrithviSaarthi addresses this challenge by providing an interactive dashboard for simplified climate data exploration and insight generation.
